Ingredients

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2 slice whole wheat bread
  • 1 slice cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium, apply olive oil.
  2. Crack 2 eggs, apply to pan. Cook eggs.
  3. Toast the bread
  4. Apply eggs to bread with cheese. Done.
